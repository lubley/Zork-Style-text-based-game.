## Zork-Style text based game 

A text adventure game where instead of explore the great underground empire, like the traditional Zork game, they explore a King's Palace completing various tasks to escape.

---

## Overview

Instead of exploring the Great Underground Empire, you are trapped inside a King's Palace as a Royal Chef. The king has given you a simple request:
- Make him a burger
- 
But ofcourse nothing is ever that simple.
To escape the palace you must:

- Explore the various palace rooms
- Collect the ingredients needed to make a burger
- Feed the King's Cat (Puzzle #2)

## How to play
Commands include 
- look
- move north/south/east/west
- take <item>
- inventory
- map
- help
and more which are given to you when you type help
